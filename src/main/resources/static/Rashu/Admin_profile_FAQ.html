13.How does the design contest feature work?

Our design contest feature allows you to request a contest to find the perfect design for your project. Here's how it works:

  • You submit a request to the admin, detailing your design needs.
  • Our admin team verifies your request to ensure it aligns with our platform's guidelines.
  • Once approved, we arrange a contest where talented designers from our community can participate.
  • Designers submit their entries, and you have the opportunity to review and provide feedback.
  • After the contest period, you can select the winning design, and the designer is awarded.

14.How is a designer's average rating determined on our platform?

The average rating for a designer is calculated based on the feedback provided by clients who have placed orders with them. Here's how it works:

  • After completing a project, clients have the opportunity to rate the designer based on their experience.
  • The rating scale typically ranges from 1 to 5, with 5 being the highest.
  • The average rating for a designer is calculated by summing up all the ratings they have received and dividing it by the total number of ratings.
  • This average rating is a valuable indicator of a designer's overall performance and the quality of their work.
